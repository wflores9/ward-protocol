XLS-66 has no coverage layer

First-loss capital protects against small shortfalls. When borrower defaults exceed that coverage, vault depositors bear the full loss. Ward Protocol insures that gap.

How It Works

Automated. On-chain. Institutional-grade.

Ward Protocol operates as a fully automated insurance layer. No manual intervention. No off-chain dependencies. Every step is verifiable on the XRP Ledger.

01
🛡️

Purchase Coverage

Vault depositors purchase insurance policies. An XLS-20 NFT policy certificate is minted on-chain with coverage terms, premium rate, and expiry encoded in metadata.

02
📊

Monitor & Assess

Ward Protocol monitors XLS-66 vault events in real time via WebSocket. Risk-based premium pricing uses multi-factor assessment with 0.5x–2.0x multipliers based on vault health.

03
⚡

Automated Settlement

When a default event is detected, 9-step validation runs automatically. Valid claims trigger 48-hour escrow settlement with a community dispute window before payout.
