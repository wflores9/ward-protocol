Protocol Architecture

Ward Builds. Institution Signs. XRPL Settles.

Three principals. Zero custodians. Ward constructs unsigned transactions. The institution signs with their own keys. XRPL enforces the outcome. Ward's server is irrelevant once escrow is on-chain.

01 / SETUP
Register & Insure

Vault registered in XLS-80 domain. KYC anchored via XLS-70. Coverage minted as non-transferable XLS-20 NFT — terms immutable on-chain.

unsigned AccountSet · NFTokenMint
02 / DETECTION
Detect & Confirm

WardMonitor watches live XRPL WebSocket events. Health ratio below 1.5 confirmed across three consecutive ledger closes before firing default.

WebSocket · 3-ledger confirmation
03 / SETTLEMENT
Validate & Settle

Nine deterministic checks against live XRPL state. All nine pass → unsigned EscrowCreate returned. Institution signs. Policy NFT burns atomically on payout.

PREIMAGE-SHA-256 · NFTokenBurn
